Send Contact Form 7, WPForms, Elementor, Ninja Forms, Contact Form Entries Plugin and many other contact form submissions to infusionsoft/Keap.

== Description ==

Contact Form 7 Keap/infusionsoft Plugin sends form submissions from [Contact Form 7](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-7/), [WPforms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/wpforms-lite/), [Elementor Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/elementor/), [Ninja Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/ninja-forms/), [Formidable Forms](https://wordpress.org/plugins/formidable/), [Contact Form Entries](https://wordpress.org/plugins/contact-form-entries/) and many other popular contact form plugins to Infusionsoft/Keap CRM when anyone submits a form. Learn more at [crmperks.com](https://www.crmperks.com/plugins/contact-form-plugins/contact-form-infusionsoft-plugin/?utm_source=wordpress&utm_medium=directory&utm_campaign=infusionsoft_readme)

== Contact Form 7 Keap/Infusionsoft Setup ==

* Go to "infusionsoft Accounts" tab and add new account then connect your Keap/infusionsoft account.
* Go to "infusionsoft Feeds" tab then add new feed.
* Map required infusionsoft fields to Contact form 7 form fields.
* Send your test entry to infusionsoft crm.
* Go to "Infusionsoft Logs" tab and verify, if entry was sent to Keap/Infusionsoft.

**Connect infusionsoft account**

Connect any contact form 7 form to infusionsoft/Keap by safe and secure Oauth 2.0. Additionally, You can connect multiple Keap/Infusionsoft accounts.

**Map infusionsoft fields**

Map any contact form 7 form fields to infusionsoft/Keap object(Contacts, Company, Tasks, Order) fields. Also, You can map unlimited Keap/infusionsoft object fields to contact form 7 form fields.

**Filter contact form 7  submissions**

Easily filter contact form 7 entries sent to infusionsoft/Keap based on user input. For example , only send contact form entry to infusionsoft which contanins work email address.

**Manually send entry to infusionsoft**

Send contact form 7 entries to infusionsoft when anyone submits a form. Later You can manually send contact form 7 entries to infusionsoft.

**infusionsoft logs**

View a detailed log of each contact form 7 entry whether sent or not sent to infusionsoft and easily resend contact form 7 entry to infusionsoft CRM.

**Send Data As infusionsoft object Notes**

Send one to many contact form 7 fields as infusionsoft Contact notes.


**Create Or Update Contact in Infusionsoft**

If a contact already exists in infusionsoft CRM, update it otherwise create a new contact.

=  Contact Form 7 Keap/infusionsoft Integration =

Easily integrate contact form 7 form to infusionsoft/Keap crm by simply connecting your infusionsoft/Keap account to then select any object and map contact form 7 fields to infusionsoft/Keap fields.

= WPForms infusionsoft =

* Easily connect WPforms to your Keap/infusionsoft account.
* Create feeds for Keap Contacts, Companies etc.
* New WPforms submission will be automatically sent to your Keap account.

= Elementor forms Keap =

* Easily Connect Elementor forms to your Keap account.
* Create a feed for any object, select correct Elementor form and finally map fields in feed.
* When someone submits Elementor form, submission will be automatically sent to your Keap account.
* You can manually send any Elementor forms entry to Keap by clicking "send to Keap" button.

= Contact form 7 Keap/infusionsoft add on =

With Contact form 7 Keap/infusionsoft addon, You can easily create a contact/company or Invoice in infusionsoft/Keap When anyone submits a contact form on your site.

= Ninja Forms infusionsoft =

* Easily connect Ninja Forms to your Keap account.
* After connecting Keap account, create a feed for Contacts, Companies and Tickets etc.
* New Ninja Forms submission will be automatically sent to your Keap account.
* You can manually send any Ninja Forms entry to Keap by clicking "send to Keap" button.

= Formidable Forms Keap =

* Connect your Keap account with our plugin.
* Create feeds for any Keap object like Contacts, Companies etc. Select correct Formidable Form in feed. 
* New Formidable Form submission will be automatically sent to your Keap account.
* You can manually send any Formidable Form entry to Keap by clicking "send to Keap" button.
